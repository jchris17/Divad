The BIT Club aims to be the premier student organization at Virginia Tech for IT related business majors. Although we mainly cater to the business field, all majors are welcome to join! The main goal of our organization is to provide students with networking opportunities to potential employers and help connect them to various industries. We hosts multiple company information sessions and joint events with other student organizations.
